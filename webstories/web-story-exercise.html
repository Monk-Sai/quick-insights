Top 9 best SEO practices to manifest!

Create high-quality content that is relevant to your target audience.

This is the most important factor in SEO, and it's what will make people want to visit your website. Your content should be well-written, informative, and engaging. It should also be optimized for search engines by including relevant keywords and phrases.

Use relevant keywords throughout your website.

This includes your title tags, meta descriptions, header tags, and body text. When choosing keywords, focus on terms that your target audience is likely to search for. You can use keyword research tools to help you find the right keywords.
